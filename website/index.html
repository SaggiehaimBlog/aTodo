The problem you face

You have an AI agent. It can read your inbox, plan your day, execute tasks, send emails, build things. It's fast. It's capable. But it moves at superhuman speed.

By the time you've had coffee, your agent has created 20 tasks. You don't know what it did, what it plans to do, what needs your decision. You're drowning in information from a tool that's supposed to help.

You need a way to stay in sync. To triage. To decide what you do, what your agent does. To see what's happening. To keep your human judgment in the loop while your agent runs at scale.

AToDo is your synchronization point. Your agent creates tasks. You review once a day. Decide. Delegate. Your agent executes. Everything is visible, everything is under your control.
